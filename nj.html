New Jersey

100.41.0.152



New Jersey, also known as the "Garden State," is a diversified and active state in the United States' northeastern area. It is well-known for its closeness to major urban centers such as New York City and Philadelphia, which makes it a popular destination for commuters and cultural exchanges. The state's natural beauty is diverse, with stunning beaches along the Jersey Shore and lush forests in the northwest. New Jersey is also known for its historical significance, featuring landmarks such as Princeton University and Trenton. Furthermore, it has a thriving arts scene, good food, and a variety of recreational activities, making it an appealing destination for both inhabitants and visitors.



Fun Fact: Frank Sinatra was born in Hoboken, New Jersey.
