New York, sometimes known as the "Big Apple," is a dynamic and iconic metropolis on the east coast of the United States. New York City is a global financial, fashion, art, and entertainment metropolis known for its towering buildings, bustling streets, and diverse culture. The city offers a varied tapestry of experiences, from the dazzling lights of Times Square to the historic architecture of areas like SoHo and Harlem. Central Park is a peaceful haven in the midst of the city, and the Statue of Liberty is a symbol of liberty and optimism. With its world-class museums, Broadway theaters, and a melting pot of cuisines, New York remains a city that never sleeps, encouraging both visitors and locals to explore its many attractions.



Fun Fact: There are estimated to be more rats than humans in New York City.
